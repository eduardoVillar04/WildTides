Wild tides is an arcade boat game about traveling from port to port on a small archipelago. 
Your main goal is reaching the maximum score before being shunken by the evil pirates or the scary tentacles, but don't worry, as you have your trusty air canon to push them into each other. 
As time goes on, the difficulty rises, but keep sailing and shooting till you can no more.
